Where Is This?

This is my blog. So, welcome! It's also something of a professional portfolio, with fiction writing and web development samples. There are also random thought salads here and there, because why not. It's my site.

Who Are You?

I'm Marc Rivers. I'm also Mary's fiancé. I grew up in and lived most of my life in Louisville, Kentucky (moving back and forth between here and other places on occasion). I'm a lifelong fan of fantasy stories in book, visual, and video game media. Writing fiction has long been a hobby, and now I'm fighting the Sisyphean battle against my own reserved nature to share it with the rest of the world.

I currently live in Louisville with a 12-pound housecat named after a Norse dragon.

What Do You Do?

I write fantasy fiction. My stories are predominantly set in the world of Estéa, where magic, wars, and migrations have shaped the land. I like to focus on heroes, hapless victims, and the occasional streetwise opportunist as they brave a dangerous land.

I'm currently working toward publishing my first novel, "Songs of the Eyldrmen".

I also do woodworking, painting miniature figurines, and playing tabletop RPGs when I can find a group for once. Poke around the site for some examples!

How Do I Contact You?
